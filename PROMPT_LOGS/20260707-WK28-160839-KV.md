**2. Research vs Plans — my recommendation**

Yes, they should be differentiated — but keep the distinction based on **commitment level**, not topic. You already have `docs/plans/` with `drupal-install.md` and `multi-tenancy.md` — both committed roadmaps. So:

| Folder | Meaning | Contents |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `docs/research/` | "I'm exploring — no decision yet" | Investigation notes, options analysis, "what would it take to..." |
| `docs/plans/` | "This is happening — here's how" | Committed roadmap items with concrete next actions |

**Graduation workflow:** when research becomes a decided direction, `git mv docs/research/X.md docs/plans/X.md` and prune the "options considered" sections. The move itself is the commit that captures the moment of decision — clean audit trail.

`NodejsResearch.md` looks like classic research: extension proposal, still exploring feasibility. Move to `docs/research/nodejs-tier.md` (matches the naming in the zip).
