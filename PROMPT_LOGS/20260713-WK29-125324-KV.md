## The DST wrinkle on "2 AM CST"

EventBridge cron uses UTC and doesn't follow DST. So "2 AM Central time" year-round isn't directly expressible — the actual wall-clock hour drifts by 1 hour between CST (winter, UTC-6) and CDT (summer, UTC-5). Two choices:

- **`cron(0 8 * * ? *)`** = 08:00 UTC = 2 AM CST in winter, 3 AM CDT in summer
- **`cron(0 7 * * ? *)`** = 07:00 UTC = 1 AM CST in winter, 2 AM CDT in summer

Since you said "CST" specifically, the literal reading is 08:00 UTC (2 AM in your winter timezone). Since we're in CDT right now, that fires at 3 AM local — still off-peak. Nobody's poking at the jumpbox at 3 AM either way.

**I'll set 08:00 UTC unless you say otherwise.** If you want to prioritize "2 AM in summer" instead (because that's most of the year), we'd use 07:00 UTC. Your call — just confirm which reading.

## On /etc customization ("isn't this sourced from somewhere at image creation?")

Good instinct to check. The jumpbox's current pipeline:
1. AWS ships you Ubuntu 24.04 ARM64 AMI (no client-specific `/etc` content)
2. **CFN UserData** runs at first boot — installs mysql-client, awscli, jq, etc. Only writes `/etc/migration-jumpbox-ready` (marker file, empty content)
3. Anything you or operators put in `/etc` after boot is what we'd need to back up

Right now `/etc/worxco/` doesn't exist on the jumpbox (that's a deploy-host convention). But the backup script will include `/etc/worxco/` in its target list — if you ever put anything there, it gets caught automatically. No harm if it doesn't exist (skip & warn per #6).

**Note:** the backup script will NOT try to capture the wider `/etc/` — that's OS-managed and would drag in tons of noise. Just `/etc/worxco/` as an operator-defined config location.

## Bucket lifecycle rules — refactor needed

The current rule expires EVERYTHING at 30 days indiscriminately. We need to make it prefix-scoped so the new `config/jumpbox-latest.tar.gz` doesn't get whacked at day 31.

**New lifecycle configuration in `cf-migration-bucket.yaml`:**

```yaml
LifecycleConfiguration:
  Rules:
    - Id: ExpireDumps
      Status: Enabled
      Prefix: dumps/
      ExpirationInDays: 30
    - Id: ExpireLogs
      Status: Enabled
      Prefix: logs/
      ExpirationInDays: 30
    - Id: ExpireScripts
      Status: Enabled
      Prefix: scripts/
      ExpirationInDays: 7          # scripts are regenerable from git; short retention
    - Id: ExpireArchivedConfig
      Status: Enabled
      Prefix: config/jumpbox-archive/
      ExpirationInDays: 90
    # NOTE: config/jumpbox-latest.tar.gz has NO expiration rule — persists indefinitely
    - Id: AbortIncompleteMultipartUpload
      Status: Enabled
      AbortIncompleteMultipartUpload:
        DaysAfterInitiation: 1
```
