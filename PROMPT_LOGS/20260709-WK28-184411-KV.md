## Quick note on `drush uli` — no harm in it

You made a fine call, but for your peace of mind so you know for tomorrow: `drush uli` doesn't touch the client's password or account. It generates a **one-time-use, time-limited login URL** (default 24 hours) for user 1 that bypasses the login form. The client's actual password is untouched — they can still log in normally with their own credentials, and the one-time link expires after use. It's essentially the same mechanism as the "forgot my password" email flow, just skipping the email step. 100% safe to use tomorrow when you want to poke around the admin side.
