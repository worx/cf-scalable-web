### Abort cascade

When any track exits non-zero:

1. The track's wrapper writes `state=вњ—` + `exit_code=N` to `status.json`.
2. Orchestrator's heartbeat loop notices.
3. **Tracks that haven't started yet:** marked `!` (aborted) and never start.
4. **Tracks already running:** **NOT killed.** Let them complete. CFN mid-flight stacks left in bad states are way worse than 25 min of wasted compute. The orchestrator just won't trigger any new tracks.
5. After all running tracks finish (one way or another), orchestrator exits non-zero with a summary.
