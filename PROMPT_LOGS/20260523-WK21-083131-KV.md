fix: DNS-propagation wait in publish-dns + clearer install-drupal-remote labels

Two operator-facing rough edges from yesterday's install-drupal-full

   returned no records — the change hadn't propagated. Two-step wait now:
   - `aws route53 wait resource-record-sets-changed` polls GetChange
