### 14:07:49 - Response

Yeah — expected behavior unfortunately. The Image Builder *pipelines* are CFN resources that got recreated yesterday (destroy-all → today's deploy-image-builder), so they have no "latest image" reference; `build-amis` triggers a fresh build cycle every time it's invoked against a freshly-recreated pipeline.

The `/3` in `1.0.10/3` is your hint: recipe `1.0.10` (content unchanged since 2026-05-18), build *number* 3 — same recipe, third bake. The AMIs from yesterday's run 2 are still in your account as "hanging chads" but Image Builder doesn't know to use them.

Future optimization (P3 territory, not now): a `build-amis-if-needed` target that queries `/sandbox/ami/<pipeline>` SSM params, fetches the AMI's Image Builder tag, compares to the current recipe version + checksum, and skips the trigger if they match. ~30 min saved on incremental deploys.
