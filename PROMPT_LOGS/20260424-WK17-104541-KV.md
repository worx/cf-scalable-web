### 10:45:49 - Response

Those are Image Builder **test instances**. After building the AMI, Image Builder launches a fresh instance from the new AMI and runs the `test` phase of the components (the verification scripts that check "is PHP installed?", "is NFS client installed?", etc.). Once the tests pass, it terminates those instances automatically.

They'll go away on their own — just wait for them to finish. Once they're gone, the AMI status will flip to `AVAILABLE` and you can proceed with `update-ami-param`.
